Section 1 — Introduction and Scope

dbappsystems.com ("Company", "we", "us", or "our") operates Load Ledger at loadledgers.com ("Service"). This Privacy Policy describes how the Company collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information submitted by users of the Service ("User", "you").

By accessing or using the Service, you acknowledge and consent to the data practices described in this Policy. This Policy is incorporated by reference into the Load Ledger Terms of Service.

Section 2 — Information Collected

The Company collects only the information necessary to provide and maintain the Service. This includes:

  • Account Registration Data: Name, email address, telephone number, company name, and commercial carrier identification numbers
  • Operational Records: Load data, dispatch records, billing documents, invoices, and financial records created or uploaded by the User
  • Technical Data: Device identifiers, browser type, IP address, and session data collected automatically during use of the Service
  • Communications: Information submitted by the User through the Customer Inquiry Form

Section 3 — Use of Information

Information collected by the Company is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the Service
  • To authenticate Users and manage account access
  • To process transactions and generate documents on behalf of the User
  • To respond to User inquiries submitted through the Customer Inquiry Form
  • To detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent or unauthorized use of the Service
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations

Section 4 — Data Security

The Company implements and maintains industry-standard technical and organizational security measures designed to protect User data against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. These measures are reviewed and updated on an ongoing basis.

No method of electronic transmission or storage is one hundred percent secure. While the Company employs reasonable safeguards, the Company cannot guarantee absolute security of User data.

Section 5 — Non-Disclosure and Non-Sale of Data

The Company does not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise transfer User personal information to third parties for commercial purposes. User data is not used for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the delivery of the Service.

Section 6 — Limited Disclosure to Third Parties

The Company may disclose User information only under the following limited circumstances:

  • To service providers engaged by the Company to assist in operating the Service, who are contractually bound to maintain confidentiality and process data only as directed by the Company
  • When required to do so by applicable law, lawful court order, or valid legal process
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or transfer of business assets, with prior notice provided to affected Users
  • With the express written consent of the User

Section 7 — Data Retention

User data is retained for the duration of the active account relationship. Upon account termination, User data is retained for thirty (30) days to facilitate any transition needs, after which it is permanently deleted from Company systems. Certain records may be retained for a longer period where required by applicable law or legitimate legal process.

Section 8 — User Rights

Subject to applicable law, Users have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information the Company holds about them
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal information
  • Request deletion of personal information, subject to legal retention obligations
  • Request a portable copy of their data in a commonly used format

To exercise any of the above rights, submit a written request through the Customer Inquiry Form at https://loadledgers.com/contact. The Company will respond within thirty (30) days of receipt. The Company will not discriminate against any User for exercising their rights under this Policy.

Section 9 — California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act, including the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to request deletion of personal information, and the right to non-discrimination for exercising these rights. California residents may submit CCPA requests through the Customer Inquiry Form at https://loadledgers.com/contact. The Company will respond within forty-five (45) days.

Section 10 — Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The Service uses cookies and similar session technologies solely to authenticate Users and maintain secure platform access. The Company does not deploy third-party advertising cookies or behavioral tracking technologies. Users may configure their browsers to decline cookies; however, doing so may impair certain Service functions.

Section 11 — Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to individuals under the age of eighteen (18). The Company does not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If the Company becomes aware that a minor has submitted personal information, such information will be promptly deleted.

Section 12 — Policy Amendments

The Company reserves the right to amend this Policy at any time. Material amendments will be communicated to registered Users prior to the effective date. The current version of this Policy is at all times available at loadledgers.com/privacy.
